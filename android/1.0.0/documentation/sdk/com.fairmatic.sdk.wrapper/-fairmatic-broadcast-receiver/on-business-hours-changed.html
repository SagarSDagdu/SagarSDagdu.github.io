onBusinessHoursChanged

fun onBusinessHoursChanged(context: Context?, fairmaticShiftDetail: FairmaticShiftDetail)
Content copied to clipboard

Called whenever the business hours are fetched and updated by the Fairmatic SDK. To get the updated business hours instantly, the application must call com.fairmatic.sdk.Fairmatic.refreshBusinessHours.

Parameters

context

  • The context in which the receiver is running.

fairmaticShiftDetail

  • Information about the updated business hours represented by the FairmaticShiftDetail object.
