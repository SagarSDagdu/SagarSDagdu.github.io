associateVehicle

fun associateVehicle(context: Context, vehicleInfo: FairmaticVehicleInfo): FairmaticVehicleTaggingOperationResult
Content copied to clipboard

Associate a vehicle with the SDK. Applications which want to tag a user's drives with the vehicle driven may call this. All drives (automatically detected or manually started) will be tagged with the vehicleId of an associated vehicle if the device was connected to the bluetooth of the vehicle during the drive. If a drive is already ongoing when this call is made, that drive will get tagged with the vehicleId if the vehicle was found connected after the call is made and before the end of the drive.

Only ten vehicles may be associated at a time.

The application must call com.fairmatic.sdk.wrapper.FairmaticVehicleTagging.dissociateVehicle when it wants to remove the vehicle.

@param context - The context of the enclosing application. @param vehicleInfo - The information of the vehicle to be associated with the Fairmatic SDK. Cannot be null. @return The result of the associateVehicle operation.
